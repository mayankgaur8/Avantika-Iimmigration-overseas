Work Visa and Job Search Pack
=============================

Resume checklist
- Use a clean international format
- Focus on measurable outcomes, not only responsibilities
- Tailor headline and summary to the target role and country

Job offer readiness
- Keep passport, degree, and experience proof ready
- Prepare notice period details and salary expectations
- Verify whether licensing or local registration is required

Employer-side documents to watch
- Contract or offer letter
- Sponsorship or permit support letter
- Company registration proof if requested

Interview preparation prompts
- Why do you want to work in this country?
- How does your experience fit the role?
- What is your relocation timeline?
- Can you explain any profile gaps clearly?

Visa-stage reminders
- Match duties to occupation code
- Keep salary and title consistent across documents
- Prepare dependent documentation early if family will travel later

Next step
Use this pack with a work visa consultation to decide whether you should prioritize job search support, employer sponsorship, or country-specific eligibility first.
