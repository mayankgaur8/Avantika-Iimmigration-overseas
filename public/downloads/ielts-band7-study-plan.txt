IELTS Band 7 Weekly Study Plan
==============================

Week 1
- Baseline mock test
- Build an error log by module
- Focus on timing discipline in reading and listening

Week 2
- Task 1 and Task 2 writing structures
- Speaking drills with cue card timing
- One full reading and listening set every other day

Week 3
- Advanced paraphrasing and idea sequencing
- Listening distractor review
- Two full speaking mocks with feedback

Week 4
- Two complete tests under exam timing
- Review only repeated error patterns
- Reduce new material and stabilize performance

Daily split
- Reading: 45 minutes
- Listening: 30 minutes
- Writing: 45 minutes
- Speaking: 20 minutes
- Review log: 15 minutes

Band 7 priorities
- Accuracy over quantity
- Structured writing over complicated writing
- Natural fluency over memorized answers

Next step
Combine this plan with expert feedback if your current score is below the target required for admissions or PR.
