Canada PR Document Checklist
============================

1. Identity
- Valid passport for all applicants
- Birth certificate if available
- Marriage certificate if applicable

2. Education
- Degree and diploma certificates
- Academic transcripts
- Educational Credential Assessment report

3. Language
- IELTS or CELPIP scorecard
- Test booking proof if result is pending

4. Work History
- Employer reference letters with duties
- Salary slips and tax records
- Offer letters or experience certificates

5. Funds and Compliance
- Six months of bank statements
- Proof of funds summary
- Police clearance certificates
- Upfront medical planning checklist

Common risk areas
- Job duties do not align with selected NOC
- Reference letters are generic or incomplete
- Proof of funds is borrowed and poorly documented
- Expired passports or outdated language scores
